What's changing

  • Kill the floating pill — bottom-right "Suggestions" widget is removed; nothing floating over content anymore.
  • Bell becomes the single hub — badge counts notifications + critical actions. Tabs: All · Actions · Bookings · Plans · Messages.
  • Actions tab replaces the pill panel: summary chips on top (urgent/pending/upcoming/HRDF), then per-action cards with the existing color-coded icons and deadline progress bars.
  • Auto-open on critical keeps working — but opens the bell dropdown on the Actions tab, not a floating panel.
  • Poll cadence stays the same; we just merge into one dropdown. NotificationBell becomes a small state holder that fetches both endpoints.
